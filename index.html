About us

  • New guild being created on Twisting Nether-EU after ToS progression.
  • Founded by motivated and experienced people.
  • Built from the ground up to be a strong community of friends.
  • Strong leadership coupled with a democracy where everyone get their voice heard.

Raiding

  • Raiding 3 days a week to clear both heroic and mythic content.
  • 3.5 hour raids with a 5 minute break (Wed / Sun / Mon).
  • Comfort range for first tier world ranking is in the 150-250.
  • Splitruns the first two weeks of each tier.

Who are we looking for?

  • Motivated people who want to help build something great from the ground up.
  • Vocal and social people that are able to talk both during and outside of raids.
  • Excellent people of all classes and roles to build a strong raiding team.
  • Dedicated people who not only log for raids, but strive for their character to be at peak performance.

What we need from you

  • Extensive raiding experience across multiple expansions.
  • Willingness to do what it takes to excel with your characters.
  • One alt with game knowledge and gear to clear heroic in splitruns.
  • Eagerness to join an active community both inside and outside of the game.
